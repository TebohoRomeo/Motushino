FEATURES

When minimalising downtime is the name of the game, keeping your HINO on the road is the best value-adding proposition. The new 700 Series integrates next-gen active safety interventions, such as:

A pre-collision system

Detects objects ahead and issues a warning before automatically applying the brakes

Adaptive cruise control (ACC)

Maintains a consistently safe distance to the vehicle in front

Lane departure warning

The driver is alerted on the display when deviating from the current driving lane

Stagger warning.

When staggering is detected, an alert is sounded to ensure timeous corrective action
